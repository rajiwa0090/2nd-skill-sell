2. Steps of Purchasing items from a shopping center(A Story Board)

Storyboard Title: Trip to the Center

Scene 1: The protagonist, Sarah, walks into a store and picks up a dress. She looks at the price tag and puts it back on the rack, feeling disappointed.

Scene 2: Sarah walks out of the store and heads to the food court, feeling hungry. She orders a meal from a fast-food restaurant and sits down at a table to eat.

Scene 3: As Sarah eats her meal, she notices a group of people carrying shopping bags from a nearby store. Curious, she decides to check it out.

Scene 4: Sarah walks into the store and browses through the aisles. She picks up a pair of shoes and tries them on. She likes them and decides to buy them.

Scene 5: Sarah heads to the checkout counter to pay for her purchase. The cashier scans the shoes, and Sarah hands over her credit card to pay for them.

Scene 6: After paying for her purchase, Sarah walks out of the store, feeling satisfied with her new shoes. She continues to walk around the shopping center, enjoying the atmosphere and browsing in other stores.

Scene 7: Sarah finds a store selling the dress she saw earlier. She looks at the price tag again but this time, she decides to buy it. She picks up the dress and heads to the checkout counter.

Scene 8: Sarah pays for the dress with her credit card and leaves the store, feeling happy with her purchases.

Sarah walks out of the shopping center, carrying her shopping bags and feeling pleased with her successful shopping trip. The camera fades to black.
